Biography

Rajat Patidar, born on June 1, 1993, in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, is a talented Indian cricketer known for his solid batting skills. He made a mark with his performances in domestic cricket and the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Career Highlights

  • IPL Debut: Played for Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB).
  • Memorable Knock: Scored a century in the IPL 2022 Eliminator, helping RCB secure a crucial victory.
  • Domestic Cricket: Represents Madhya Pradesh in Ranji Trophy.
