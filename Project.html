Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team, known for his aggressive style and incredible consistency. Born on November 5, 1988, he has become one of the most successful and influential players in the history of cricket.

Career Highlights

  • Fastest player to 10,000 ODI runs
  • First player to score 7 double centuries as a captain
  • Over 70 international centuries
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times
