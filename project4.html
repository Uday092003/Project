Biography

Yash Dayal, born on December 13, 1997, in Allahabad (Prayagraj), Uttar Pradesh, is an emerging left-arm fast bowler. He is known for his ability to swing the ball and deliver under pressure. He gained attention with his performances in domestic cricket and the Indian Premier League (IPL).

Career Highlights

  • Domestic Cricket: Represents Uttar Pradesh in Ranji Trophy.
  • IPL Debut: Signed by Gujarat Titans in IPL 2022.
  • Memorable Performance: Played a crucial role in the Gujarat Titans' successful IPL 2022 campaign.
