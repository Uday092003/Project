Biography

Mohammad Siraj, born on March 13, 1994, in Hyderabad, India, rose from humble beginnings to become one of India's premier fast bowlers. Known for his hard work, determination, and lethal pace, he represents a new generation of Indian pacers.

Career Highlights

  • Test Debut: December 2020 against Australia.
  • Memorable Performance: Took a five-wicket haul at the Gabba, helping India win the Border-Gavaskar Trophy in 2021.
  • IPL: A key bowler for Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB).
  • ODIs: Delivered consistent performances, particularly in the Asia Cup 2023 Final.
