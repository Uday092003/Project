Royal Challengers Bangalore

About RCB

Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a franchise cricket team that represents Bengaluru in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Known for its passionate fanbase and star players, RCB has made it to multiple finals but is still in search of their maiden IPL title.

Key Players

  • Virat Kohli
  • Rajat Patidar
  • Mohammed Siraj
